## üìö What is this project ?

***Forsaken Planet*** is a serious game developed in Godot 4.3, focused on the theme of climate change. In the game, players will explore various environments on a mysterious planet, solving puzzles and uncovering clues about the disappearance of an ancient civilization that once thrived there.

As **Quireo**, an explorer from the planet Eradon, your mission is to uncover the fate of the ancient civilization and gather crucial information to prevent similar catastrophic events from occurring on your home planet. Along the way, you will be assisted by your colleague and friend, **Thothan**, a brilliant scientist whose knowledge and insight will be vital in your quest for answers.

## üéÆ Gameplay Overview

In *Forsaken Planet*, players will embark on a journey to uncover the mysteries of a planet abandoned by its inhabitants.

* **Exploration**: Navigate through diverse biomes, each presenting unique challenges, environmental puzzles, and hidden lore.
* **Puzzles**: Solve intricate puzzles inspired by real-world environmental issues, offering insight into sustainable solutions.
* **Narrative**: Experience a rich story that merges ecological awareness with a compelling sci-fi adventure.
* **Companion Dynamics**: Collaborate with Thothan to analyze findings, solve puzzles, and uncover the secrets of the planet‚Äôs downfall.

## üåü Features

* **Immersive Environments**: Lush forests, barren wastelands, and technologically advanced ruins brought to life with Godot's 4.3 rendering capabilities.
* **Dynamic Interaction**: Real-time dialogue and task coordination with Thothan to solve problems.
* **Educational Elements**: Subtle learning moments designed to challenge perceptions about climate change and foster critical thinking.

## üìà Project Objectives

We actively want to develop a game that blends **entertainment with impactful ecological education**, encourage players to reflect on climate change and consider actionable solutions as well as deliver an engaging narrative that **resonates with both casual and avid gamers**.

## üé® Art and Design

The visual assets for *Forsaken Planet* were created with an emphasis on environmental storytelling.

* **Inspiration**: Flora and fauna inspired by real-world ecosystems under threat.
* **Design Tools**: Created using [**Aseprite**](https://aseprite.org) for pixel art.
* **Soundtrack**: Created using [**Suno**](https://suno.com) that enhance the immersive experience.
